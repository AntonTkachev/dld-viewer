About Um Suqaim Third

Um Suqaim Third is a Dubai district. All transactions are apartments; villas and townhouses are not built here. About 69% are off-plan purchases in under-construction projects. Most common type — 1-bedroom (2,147 transactions). One of the most active projects — Jumeirah Living Marsa Al Arab. 10,234 rental contracts have also been recorded — the district is attractive for both purchase and long-term living.
