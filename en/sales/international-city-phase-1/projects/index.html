About International City Phase 1

International City Phase 1 is a Dubai district. All transactions are apartments; villas and townhouses are not built here. About 9% are off-plan purchases in under-construction projects. Most common type — studios (20,138 transactions). One of the most active projects — International City Emarati. 367,520 rental contracts have also been recorded — the district is attractive for both purchase and long-term living.
