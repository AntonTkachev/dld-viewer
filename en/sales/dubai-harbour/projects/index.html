About Dubai Harbour

Dubai Harbour is a Dubai district. All transactions are apartments; villas and townhouses are not built here. About 83% are off-plan purchases in under-construction projects. Most common type — 2-bedroom (5,224 transactions). One of the most active projects — Damac Bay. 2,823 rental contracts have also been recorded — the district is attractive for both purchase and long-term living.
