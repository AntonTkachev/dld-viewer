About Jaddaf Waterfront

Jaddaf Waterfront is a Dubai district. All transactions are apartments; villas and townhouses are not built here. About 56% are off-plan purchases in under-construction projects. Most common type — 1-bedroom (1,839 transactions). One of the most active projects — D1. 13,050 rental contracts have also been recorded — the district is attractive for both purchase and long-term living.
