About City Walk

City Walk is a Dubai district. All transactions are apartments; villas and townhouses are not built here. About 60% are off-plan purchases in under-construction projects. Most common type — 2-bedroom (3,441 transactions). One of the most active projects — No.1, No.2A, No.2B, No.3A And 3B Citywalk Residential. 9,380 rental contracts have also been recorded — the district is attractive for both purchase and long-term living.
