About EXPO CITY

EXPO CITY is a Dubai district. All transactions are apartments; villas and townhouses are not built here. About 100% are off-plan purchases in under-construction projects. Most common type — 2-bedroom (1,920 transactions). One of the most active projects — Terra Heights. 4,269 rental contracts have also been recorded — the district is attractive for both purchase and long-term living.
