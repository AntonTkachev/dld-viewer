About DMCC Master Community

DMCC Master Community is a Dubai district. All transactions are apartments; villas and townhouses are not built here. About 23% are off-plan purchases in under-construction projects. Most common type — other (30,359 transactions). One of the most active projects — Al Mas Tower. 155,620 rental contracts have also been recorded — the district is attractive for both purchase and long-term living.
