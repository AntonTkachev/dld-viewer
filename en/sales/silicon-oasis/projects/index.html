About Silicon Oasis

Silicon Oasis is a Dubai district. All transactions are apartments; villas and townhouses are not built here. About 32% are off-plan purchases in under-construction projects. Most common type — 1-bedroom (12,591 transactions). One of the most active projects — Palace Tower. 198,572 rental contracts have also been recorded — the district is attractive for both purchase and long-term living.
