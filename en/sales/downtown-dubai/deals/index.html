About DownTown Dubai

DownTown Dubai is a Dubai district. All transactions are apartments; villas and townhouses are not built here. About 33% are off-plan purchases in under-construction projects. Most common type — 1-bedroom (27,821 transactions). One of the most active projects — Burj Khalifa Towers. 96,983 rental contracts have also been recorded — the district is attractive for both purchase and long-term living.
