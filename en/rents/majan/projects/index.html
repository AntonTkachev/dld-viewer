About Majan

Majan is a Dubai district. All transactions are apartments; villas and townhouses are not built here. About 69% are off-plan purchases in under-construction projects. Most common type — studios (5,123 transactions). One of the most active projects — Binghatti Vintage. 22,726 rental contracts have also been recorded — the district is attractive for both purchase and long-term living.
