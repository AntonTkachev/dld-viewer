About Dubai Health Care City Phase 2

Dubai Health Care City Phase 2 is a Dubai district. All transactions are apartments; villas and townhouses are not built here. About 63% are off-plan purchases in under-construction projects. Most common type — 1-bedroom (3,397 transactions). One of the most active projects — Keturah Resort. 8,034 rental contracts have also been recorded — the district is attractive for both purchase and long-term living.
