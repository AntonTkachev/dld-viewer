About Meydan Racecourse Community

Meydan Racecourse Community is a Dubai district. All transactions are apartments; villas and townhouses are not built here. About 43% are off-plan purchases in under-construction projects. Most common type — 1-bedroom (2,551 transactions). One of the most active projects — The Polo Residence. 12,113 rental contracts have also been recorded — the district is attractive for both purchase and long-term living.
